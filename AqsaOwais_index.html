How to Get Started

Step 1

Find a Cause That You Are Passionate About

Although this may see simple in theory, in reality, this requires extensive soul-searching. Unless you are truly passionate about what you are doing, you will never create significant change. Moreover, instead of finding joy in the what that you do accomplish, you will view it as tedious tasks you have to do.

Step 2

Create a Plan of Action

Once you have a cause that you are willing to devote hours on end to advocate in favor of, you need to create a plan of action. Your plan of action will be the steps you will take to best advocate for your cause by optimizing your skill set.

A plan of action differs by person and there is definitely no right or wrong way to go about it. If you are just getting started or looking for a group of like-minded individuals you can join a club or organization that focuses on your cause, or maybe even start your own. If you are a really great coder, you can make a website for your cause. Or if you are really great at writing poems, you can write poetry that highlights your cause. As you can see, the list is truly endless, and no matter what you choose to do, you can make a difference.

Step 3

Make a Difference

Now that you have a solid plan of action, it is time for you to make a difference. Go out there and do your best, because anyone, anywhere can change the world and make it a better place for us all! Good luck!
